### Treatment Options for Essential Tremor

The **first-line pharmacologic treatments** for essential tremor are:
- **Propranolol** (a non-selective beta-blocker)
- **Primidone** (an anticonvulsant)

Both have demonstrated efficacy, but **propranolol is generally considered the first-line** due to its efficacy and tolerability in many patients.

However, in patients with **asthma**, **non-selective beta-blockers like propranolol are contraindicated**. Propranolol can **worsen bronchospasm** by blocking beta-2 receptors in the airways, potentially triggering a life-threatening asthma exacerbation.
