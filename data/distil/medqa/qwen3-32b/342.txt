### Understanding Hepatocellular Carcinoma (HCC)

HCC is the most common type of primary liver cancer and typically develops in the setting of **chronic liver disease**, especially **cirrhosis**. Major risk factors for HCC include:

- **Chronic hepatitis B or C infection**
- **Alcoholic liver disease**
- **Non-alcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH)**
- **Aflatoxin exposure**
- **Hemochromatosis**
- **Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency**
- **Diabetes**
- **Obesity**
