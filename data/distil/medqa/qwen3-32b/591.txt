### Role of Imaging in Pulmonary Nodules

- **Chest X-ray:** While it detected the lesion, it is not ideal for detailed characterization of small nodules. It lacks the resolution to determine features such as spiculation, calcification, or growth over time.
- **CT Scan of the Chest:** This is the **gold standard** for evaluating pulmonary nodules. It provides better anatomical detail and can help distinguish between benign and malignant features (e.g., smooth vs. irregular borders, solid vs. ground-glass morphology).
