### Etiology of Epididymitis in Adolescents

In adolescents and young adults, **sexually transmitted infections (STIs)** are the most common cause of epididymitis, particularly:

- **Chlamydia trachomatis**
- **Neisseria gonorrhoeae**

These organisms often present with **urethritis** and can ascend to the epididymis, causing inflammation. **Nucleic acid amplification testing (NAAT)** is the gold standard for detecting these organisms in **urine or urethral swabs**.
