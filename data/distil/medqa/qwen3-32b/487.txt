The **gold standard** for diagnosing **COPD** is **spirometry**, which measures **FEV1/FVC ratio**. A **post-bronchodilator FEV1/FVC < 0.7** confirms the diagnosis of airflow obstruction. This is the **first-line and most specific test** in this clinical context.
