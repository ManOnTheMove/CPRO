### Management of Sunburn

The treatment of a **mild to moderate sunburn** is primarily **supportive** and includes:

- **Cooling the skin** (e.g., cool compresses or damp gauze).
- **Hydration** (oral fluids, not necessarily IV unless severe).
- **Pain relief** (e.g., NSAIDs or topical anesthetics like lidocaine).
- **Topical soothing agents** such as **aloe vera**, which has anti-inflammatory and moisturizing properties.
- **Avoidance of further sun exposure** and use of sunscreen in the future.
