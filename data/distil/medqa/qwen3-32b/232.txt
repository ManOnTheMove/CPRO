The question asks what a **biopsy of one of the hyperfunctioning nodules** would most likely show. Let's evaluate the most likely histopathological finding in the context of a **hyperfunctioning follicular cell nodule**, such as a **toxic adenoma** or **toxic multinodular goiter**.

- Histologically, these nodules are characterized by **crowded follicular cells** and **scalloped colloid**, as the follicles are filled with colloid that is pushed outward by the proliferating epithelium.
