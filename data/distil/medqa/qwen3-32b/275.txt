Given the **bilateral, symmetric, progressive hearing loss**, **normal otoscopic findings**, and **normal tuning fork results**, the most likely diagnosis is:

  - **Difficulty hearing high-pitched sounds** (e.g., consonants like *s*, *f*, *th*)
