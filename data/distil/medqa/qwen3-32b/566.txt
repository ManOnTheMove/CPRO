A 2-year-old boy presents with a **severe cough**, **vomiting**, **"whoop" sound** during inhalation, and **petechiae** on his face. ### Treatment of Pertussis in Children

The **Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)** and other pediatric infectious disease guidelines recommend **macrolide antibiotics** as the **first-line treatment** for pertussis in **children under 8 years old**, particularly infants and toddlers, due to the severity of the disease in this age group.

The **first-line agents** include:
- **Erythromycin**
- **Azithromycin**
- **Clarithromycin**

These antibiotics are effective against *Bordetella pertussis* and are preferred due to their **targeted activity** and **relative safety** in young children.
