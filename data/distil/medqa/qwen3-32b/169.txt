- **Class II**: Beta blockers (e.g., Metoprolol, Carvedilol)

- **Class III**: **Potassium channel blockers** – These **prolong the action potential duration and the QT interval**, and **increase the effective refractory period** (e.g., **Amiodarone**, **Sotalol**, **Dofetilide**)

- **Class IV**: Calcium channel blockers (e.g., **Diltiazem**, Verapamil)
