Key features of **Wallenberg syndrome** include:

- **Ipsilateral**:
  - Ataxia (from inferior cerebellar peduncle involvement)
  - Horner’s syndrome (ptosis, miosis) from sympathetic pathway disruption
  - Vestibular symptoms (nystagmus, vertigo)

### Differentiating from Other Vascular Territories

- **Anterior inferior cerebellar artery (AICA)** infarcts affect the **pons** and **anterior cerebellum**.
