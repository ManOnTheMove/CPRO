### Pharmacologic Mechanism of Prazosin

Prazosin is a **selective alpha-1 adrenergic receptor antagonist**. Its mechanism of action includes:

1. **Relaxation of smooth muscle in the prostate and bladder neck** – this helps alleviate **BPH-related urinary symptoms** such as hesitancy and reduced flow.
2. **Vasodilation of peripheral blood vessels** – this leads to a **reduction in peripheral vascular resistance**, which is beneficial in the management of **hypertension**.

Importantly, prazosin **does not significantly affect cardiac contractility** or **renin release**. These effects are more characteristic of beta-blockers.
