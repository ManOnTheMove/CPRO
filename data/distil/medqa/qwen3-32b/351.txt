- **Interstitial Cystitis or Irritable Bowel Syndrome**: May contribute to pelvic pain, but typically not cyclical.
- **Uterine Fibroids or Ovarian Cysts**: May cause pain, but would likely be detectable on pelvic exam or imaging.
