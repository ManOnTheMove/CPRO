3. **Mid-diastolic murmur** at the lower left sternal border:
   - This is a **tricuspid flow murmur**, caused by increased blood flow through the tricuspid valve due to right atrial volume overload in ASD.
   - This is a **low-pitched**, **soft** murmur and is a common finding in ASD.
