### Understanding Cervical Cancer Staging

According to **FIGO (Fédération Internationale de Gynécologie et d'Obstétrique)** staging:

- **Stage IIA**: Tumor extends to the **upper two-thirds of the vagina**, **without parametrial invasion**.

- **Stage IIIB**: Tumor extends to the **pelvic wall** and/or causes **ureteral obstruction**, resulting in **hydronephrosis or non-functioning kidney**. **Involvement of the lower third of the vagina** is also included in this stage.
- **Stage IVA**: Involves **bladder or rectal mucosa**.
- **Stage IVB**: **Distant metastases** are present.
