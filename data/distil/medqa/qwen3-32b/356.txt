### Chromosomal Translocations in AML:

AML is a heterogeneous group of leukemias with various genetic abnormalities. Among them, **chromosomal translocations** are important for classification, prognosis, and treatment decisions.

- **t(15;17)** → **Acute Promyelocytic Leukemia (APL)**

- **t(11;14)** → **Mantle cell lymphoma**, a lymphoid malignancy.
- **t(8;14)** → **Burkitt lymphoma**, again a lymphoid malignancy.
