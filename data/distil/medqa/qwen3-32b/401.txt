### Maternal Serum Screening and Neural Tube Defects

During pregnancy, **maternal serum alpha-fetoprotein (AFP)** is a key biochemical marker used in screening for **open neural tube defects**, such as **myelomeningocele**, a severe form of spina bifida. In such cases, the **fetus leaks AFP into the amniotic fluid**, which in turn elevates the **maternal serum AFP level**. This elevation is a strong indicator of an open NTD.
