### Key Diagnostic Consideration

The constellation of symptoms—**macroglossia, neonatal hypoglycemia, and overgrowth**—strongly suggests **Beckwith-Wiedemann Syndrome (BWS)**, a pediatric overgrowth disorder associated with **genomic imprinting abnormalities**, particularly on **chromosome 11p15.5**.

- **Macroglossia**
- **Neonatal hypoglycemia**
- **Macrosomia at birth**
- **Omphalocele**
