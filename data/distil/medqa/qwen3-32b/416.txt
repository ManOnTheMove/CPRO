### Therapeutic Implications

The primary goal in **methanol or ethylene glycol poisoning** is to **inhibit the metabolism** of the parent alcohol into its toxic metabolites (formic acid or glycolic/oxalic acid), which cause metabolic acidosis, visual toxicity, and renal failure.
