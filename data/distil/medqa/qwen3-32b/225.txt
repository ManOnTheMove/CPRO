### Virology of Hepatitis E

- **Hepatitis E virus (HEV)** is an **RNA virus**.
- It is **non-enveloped**, which distinguishes it from Hepatitis B and C (both enveloped).
- The genome is **positive-sense RNA**, meaning it can be directly translated by host ribosomes.
- It belongs to the **Hepeviridae** family, **not** the Picornaviridae family (which includes Hepatitis A virus).
