### Pharmacologic Intervention in SVT

The **first-line acute treatment** for SVT is **adenosine**, a **short-acting nucleoside** that:

- Binds to **A1 adenosine receptors** in the **atrioventricular (AV) node**.
- **Decreases intracellular cAMP**, leading to **reduced calcium influx**.
- **Slows AV nodal conduction** and **refractoriness**, which can **interrupt reentrant circuits**, especially in **AV nodal reentrant tachycardia (AVNRT)** or **AV reentrant tachycardia (AVRT)**.

Since **vagal maneuvers** failed in this case, **adenosine** is a **logical next step**. It is known to **rapidly restore normal sinus rhythm** in patients with SVT and is consistent with the clinical scenario.
