### Pharmacological Context

**Fenoldopam** is a **selective dopamine D1 receptor agonist**. It is used in the management of **acute hypertension** and **acute renal failure**, particularly when increased **renal blood flow** is desired.

- **D1 receptors** are **G-protein-coupled receptors (GPCRs)** that couple to **Gs protein**.
- Activation of Gs leads to **stimulation of adenylyl cyclase**, which converts **ATP to cyclic AMP (cAMP)**.
- **cAMP** is a second messenger that activates **protein kinase A (PKA)**.
- PKA promotes **vasodilation** in smooth muscle by phosphorylating target proteins that reduce intracellular calcium sensitivity or increase calcium efflux.
