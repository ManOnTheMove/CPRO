### Understanding the Tetanus Vaccine

The **tetanus vaccine** contains **inactivated tetanus toxin** (a toxoid). When this is administered, it **stimulates the immune system** to produce **antibodies against the tetanus toxin**. This is the **primary goal** of the vaccine: to **induce active immunity** by **generating anti-tetanus antibodies**.
