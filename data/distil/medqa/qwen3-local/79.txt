- **Family history of sudden cardiac death**: HCM is a genetic condition with a strong hereditary component.

### Treatment Considerations in HCM:

The **primary goal** in HCM is to **relieve symptoms**, **prevent syncope**, and **reduce the risk of sudden cardiac death**. The **first-line treatment** is **beta-blockade**, such as **metoprolol**, which:
- **Reduces heart rate** and **contractility**, thereby **decreasing LVOT gradient**.
- **Improves diastolic function**.
- **Reduces the risk of arrhythmias**.
