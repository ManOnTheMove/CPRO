### Understanding the RER Structure

The **rough endoplasmic reticulum** is a membranous network of sacs and tubules that is **studded with ribosomes**, giving it a "rough" appearance. These ribosomes are responsible for **protein synthesis**, particularly for proteins that are either secreted or embedded in membranes. The **ribosomes themselves** are composed of **ribosomal RNA (rRNA)** and **proteins**.

---

### What is a Northern Blot?

A **northern blot** is a molecular biology technique used to detect **specific RNA molecules** in a sample. It involves transferring RNA from a gel to a membrane and then hybridizing it with a labeled probe. The goal is to identify and quantify particular **nucleic acid sequences**.

---
