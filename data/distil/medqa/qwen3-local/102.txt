The **mainstay of modern HCV treatment** involves **direct-acting antivirals (DAAs)**, which target specific viral proteins to inhibit viral replication and clearance.

Common DAA targets include:

- **NS5B RNA polymerase** (e.g., sofosbuvir)
- **NS3/4A protease** (e.g., paritaprevir)
- **NS5A** (e.g., ledipasvir)
