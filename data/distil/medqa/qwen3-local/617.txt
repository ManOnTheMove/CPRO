### Key Considerations in Anaphylaxis Management

- **Epinephrine is the first-line treatment** for anaphylaxis, as it reverses airway edema, bronchoconstriction, and hypotension.

- **Intravenous (IV) epinephrine** is reserved for **patients not responding to IM epinephrine**, or in **cardiopulmonary arrest**. It requires careful titration and is typically administered in a monitored setting.
