### Key Points About 5-Fluorouracil (5-FU)

5-FU is a widely used chemotherapy drug, particularly in the treatment of colorectal cancer. Its mechanism involves inhibiting thymidylate synthase, thereby interfering with DNA synthesis in rapidly dividing cells, such as cancer cells. However, this also affects other rapidly dividing cells in the body, especially in the bone marrow, gastrointestinal tract, and skin.

- **Neutropenia** (low neutrophil count)
- **Thrombocytopenia** (low platelet count)
- **Anemia** (low red blood cell count)
