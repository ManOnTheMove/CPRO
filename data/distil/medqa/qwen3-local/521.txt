### Key Features of Hyper IgM Syndrome:
- **Immunological Defect**: Impaired B-cell differentiation and class-switching, resulting in **low IgG, IgA, and IgE**, but **elevated IgM**.

- **Long-Term Risks**: Patients with Hyper IgM Syndrome have an **increased risk of developing lymphomas and other malignancies**, likely due to chronic immune activation and dysfunction.
