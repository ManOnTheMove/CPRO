### Understanding Clubbing

Clubbing is a physical finding characterized by the softening of the nail bed, a change in the angle between the nail and the finger, and a bulbous appearance of the fingertips. It is most commonly associated with **chronic hypoxemia**, which occurs when the body tissues are consistently deprived of oxygen. This condition is often seen in patients with **chronic lung diseases**, such as interstitial lung disease, cystic fibrosis, or lung cancer.
