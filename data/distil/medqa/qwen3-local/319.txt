### **Key Considerations in Diverticulitis Management**

Diverticulitis is a condition where small pouches (diverticula) in the colon become inflamed or infected. **Complicated diverticulitis** involves **perforation, abscess formation, or obstruction**. In such cases, **antibiotics** and **drainage** are typically required.

- **Uncomplicated diverticulitis** may be managed with **supportive care and antibiotics**.
- **Complicated diverticulitis** (with abscess or perforation) requires **antibiotics and intervention**, either **percutaneous drainage** or **surgical drainage** depending on the patient's stability and the size of the abscess.
