1. **Setting**: Ensure a private and comfortable environment.
2. **Perception**: Assess the patient's understanding of their condition.
3. **Invitation**: Ask the patient how they want to receive the information.
4. **Knowledge**: Provide the information in a clear and compassionate manner.
5. **Empathy**: Acknowledge the patient's emotions.
6. **Strategy and Summary**: Discuss next steps and provide support.
