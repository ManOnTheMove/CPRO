### Understanding the Pathogen and Its Vaccine

The **Hib vaccine** was introduced to prevent invasive diseases caused by *H. influenzae* type b, including:

- **Meningitis**
- **Pneumonia**
- **Epiglottitis**
- **Bacteremia**

Although it is not a vaccine for all *Haemophilus* species, it specifically targets **type b**. Since the question asks which condition would also be prevented by vaccination against the **causal pathogen of this patient’s current disease**, we are looking for a **related condition** that is also **preventable by the Hib vaccine**.
