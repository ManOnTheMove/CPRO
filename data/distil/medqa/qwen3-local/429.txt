### Clinical Features of Giant Cell Arteritis (GCA):

- **New-onset headache** (often unilateral)
- **Scalp tenderness** (especially over the temporal arteries)
- **Jaw claudication** (pain in the jaw during chewing)
- **Visual symptoms** (such as blurred vision, diplopia, or sudden monocular blindness)
- **Fatigue, fever, and weight loss** may also be present
