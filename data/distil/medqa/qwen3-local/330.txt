### Mechanism of Cholera Toxin:
The **cholera toxin** is an **AB-type toxin** that:
- Binds to **GM1 ganglioside receptors** on the surface of intestinal epithelial cells.
- The **A subunit** enters the cell and **activates adenylate cyclase**, leading to a **massive increase in intracellular cAMP**.
- This results in **electrolyte and water secretion** into the intestinal lumen, causing **profuse, watery diarrhea**.
