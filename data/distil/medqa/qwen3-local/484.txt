This pattern is consistent with **hypercholesterolemia**, specifically **elevated LDL cholesterol**, which is a major risk factor for **cardiovascular disease**. Given his family history and the severity of his LDL levels, the most likely treatment would be a **statin**, which is the **first-line therapy** for such cases.

### Mechanism of Action of Statins:

Statins inhibit **HMG-CoA reductase**, the **rate-limiting enzyme** in the **cholesterol biosynthesis pathway**. By inhibiting this enzyme, they reduce hepatic cholesterol production, which leads to increased expression of **LDL receptors** on liver cells, thereby increasing the clearance of LDL cholesterol from the bloodstream.
