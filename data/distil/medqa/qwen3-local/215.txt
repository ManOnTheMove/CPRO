The key to the diagnosis lies in the **clinical presentation** and **underlying risk factors**.

### Key Clinical Findings:
- **Left eye fixed in a down and outward position**: This is classic for **CN III palsy**.

- **No other complaints**: No headache, seizures, or other neurological symptoms, which helps narrow the differential.

### Consideration of Underlying Causes:
