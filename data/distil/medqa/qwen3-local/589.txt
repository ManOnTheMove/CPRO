### Understanding Adalimumab and Its Mechanism

Adalimumab is a **monoclonal antibody** that targets **tumor necrosis factor-alpha (TNF-α)**, a key pro-inflammatory cytokine in autoimmune diseases such as psoriatic arthritis. However, long-term use of biologics like adalimumab can lead to **immunogenicity**, where the body generates **antidrug antibodies (ADAs)**. These antibodies can neutralize the drug, reducing its efficacy and potentially causing a **loss of response**.
