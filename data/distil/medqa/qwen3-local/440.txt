- **Non-accusatory and Supportive Communication**: The physician should avoid pressuring the patient to take specific actions (e.g., reporting to the police) and instead focus on offering support and resources.

- **Confidentiality and Trust**: The physician must maintain a safe and trusting environment for the patient to disclose sensitive information.
