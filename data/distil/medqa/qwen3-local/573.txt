### Management Considerations

In **acute adrenal insufficiency (Addisonian crisis)**, the **immediate treatment** includes:

- **Intravenous fluids** (e.g., **normal saline**) to correct **volume depletion** and **hypotension**
- **Corticosteroids** (e.g., **dexamethasone or hydrocortisone**) to replace **cortisol deficiency**
